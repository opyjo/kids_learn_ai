## 🎤 The Showcase

### What You'll Present

When it's your turn, you'll:
1. **Show your adventure game** — Run it for the class!
2. **Explain what it does** — Tell us about your story
3. **Share what you're proud of** — What makes it special?
4. **Let classmates play** — They can try different choices!

### Presentation Tips

- **Be confident** — You built something amazing!
- **Speak clearly** — Tell us about your adventure
- **Show it off** — Run it with different choices!
- **Be proud** — This is YOUR work!

### What to Say

Here's a template you can use:

> "Hi! My name is [your name], and this is my adventure game! It's about [describe your story]. I'm really proud of [what you're proud of]. Would you like to see it in action?"

Then run your adventure game and show different choices!
