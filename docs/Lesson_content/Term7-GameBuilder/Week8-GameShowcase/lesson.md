## 🎤 How to Present Your Game

### What to Include (2-3 minutes)

1. **Introduce Yourself**
   - Your name
   - What you created

2. **Show Your Welcome Screen**
   - Run your game
   - Show the welcome message

3. **Demonstrate Gameplay**
   - Play one complete round
   - Show the hints (higher/lower)
   - Show score tracking
   - Show "Play again?" feature

4. **Share What You're Proud Of**
   - What was your favorite feature?
   - What was challenging?
   - What did you learn?

### Presentation Tips

- **Speak clearly** — Take your time
- **Be proud** — You created something amazing!
- **Have fun** — This is a celebration!
- **Answer questions** — Your classmates might ask about your game

### Example Script

> "Hi! I'm [Your Name], and I created a Number Guessing Game! It picks a random number, gives hints, and tracks your score. Let me show you..."

[Run your game and demonstrate]

> "I'm really proud of [specific feature] because [reason]. The hardest part was [challenge], but I learned [what you learned]."
