# Term 5, Lesson 8: Showcase + Badge! 🎉

## Teacher's Guide

**Course:** Term 5: AI Sneak Peek  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Term:** 5 of 8 | **Week:** 8 of 8

---

## 📋 Lesson Overview

### Purpose

This is the celebration and showcase lesson where students present their AI in My Life posters, share their AI discoveries, and earn their "AI Curious" badge. This celebrates their AI learning journey and recognizes their achievement of becoming AI-literate.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will have:

1. Presented their AI in My Life posters to the class
2. Shared their AI discoveries with others
3. Viewed and celebrated other students' posters
4. Earned their "AI Curious" badge
5. Celebrated their AI learning achievements

### Materials Needed

- Computer with internet access (for presentations)
- Projector/screen share for displaying posters
- AI Curious badges (physical or digital)
- Camera for photos (optional)
- Celebration snacks (optional)
- Certificate templates (optional)

### Pre-Lesson Preparation

1. **Review student posters** — Know what each student created
2. **Prepare badges** — Physical or digital badges ready
3. **Plan presentation order** — Who presents when
4. **Prepare celebration** — Snacks, music, decorations (optional)
5. **Prepare certificates** — If using certificates
6. **Set up display area** — For posters if physical

## 📚 Detailed Teaching Guide

### Part 1: Welcome & Introduction (5 minutes)

#### Goals

- Set celebratory tone
- Explain the day's activities
- Build excitement

#### The Welcome

> "Welcome to SHOWCASE DAY! 🎉 This is YOUR day to shine! You've learned so much about AI this term, and today we're going to celebrate everything you've accomplished!"

**Explain the day:**
1. **Presentations** — Each student shares their poster
2. **Gallery Walk** — See everyone's posters
3. **Badge Ceremony** — Earn your AI Curious badge!

**Set expectations:**
- Be supportive of each other
- Ask questions
- Celebrate everyone's work
- Have fun!

#### Teaching Tips

- Be enthusiastic and celebratory
- Set positive, supportive tone
- Make it feel special
- Keep it brief—get to presentations

---

### Part 2: Presentations (30 minutes)

#### Goals

- Each student presents their poster
- Celebrate individual achievements
- Build confidence

#### Presentation Structure

**For each student (2-3 minutes):**

1. **Introduction** — Name and poster format
2. **Show Poster** — Display or hold up poster
3. **AI Examples** — Walk through AI examples found
4. **Highlights** — Favorite AI, what surprised them
5. **Question** — Share their question about AI
6. **Questions** — Answer 1-2 questions
7. **Applause** — Celebrate!

**Presentation prompts:**
- "Tell us about your poster!"
- "What AI did you find?"
- "What surprised you most?"
- "What's your question about AI?"

**Keep it positive:**
- Celebrate what they created
- Focus on achievements
- Be supportive
- Keep it brief

#### Teaching Tips

- Keep presentations short (2-3 min each)
- Help shy students
- Celebrate each presentation
- Keep energy positive
- Take notes for badges

---

### Part 3: Gallery Walk (15 minutes)

#### Goals

- Students view all posters
- Discover new AI examples
- Give positive feedback
- Have fun!

#### Gallery Walk Time

> "Now let's look at everyone's posters! Walk around, see all the amazing work, and discover new AI examples!"

**Structure:**
- Students can move around
- Look at all posters
- Give positive feedback
- Notice creativity
- Have fun!

**Encourage:**
- Find new AI examples
- Notice different formats
- Appreciate creativity
- Give compliments

#### Teaching Tips

- Let them explore freely
- Be available for help
- Encourage positive feedback
- Keep it fun and positive
- Take photos if possible

---

### Part 4: Badge Ceremony (10 minutes)

#### Goals

- Award AI Curious badges
- Recognize achievements
- Make it special

#### The Ceremony

> "You've all earned your AI Curious badge! 🤖 You learned what AI is, found it everywhere, understood how it works, and created amazing posters! You're AI-literate!"

**Award badges:**
- Call each student up
- Present badge
- Say something specific about their achievement
- Take photo (optional)
- Applause!

**What they earned:**
- ✅ Completed Term 5
- ✅ Created AI in My Life poster
- ✅ Identified 5+ AI examples
- ✅ Explained what AI does
- ✅ Presented their work

#### Teaching Tips

- Make it special and personal
- Say something specific about each student
- Take photos if possible
- Celebrate each one
- Make it memorable

---

## 🎯 Assessment & Recognition

### Badge Requirements

Students earn the AI Curious badge by:

- ✅ Completing Term 5
- ✅ Creating AI in My Life poster
- ✅ Identifying 5+ AI examples
- ✅ Explaining what AI does
- ✅ Presenting their work

### Recognition Ideas

- **Badges** — Physical or digital
- **Certificates** — Print certificates
- **Photos** — Take photos with badges
- **Shout-outs** — Recognize specific achievements
- **Display** — Show posters on screen/wall
