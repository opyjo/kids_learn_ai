## 🎮 Showcase Activities

### Part 1: Presentations (30 minutes)

Each student will present their AI in My Life poster!

**What to share:**
- Your poster format (Python, digital, or hand-drawn)
- The AI examples you found
- What each AI does
- Whether you think each AI is helpful
- Your question about AI
- What surprised you most

**How to present:**
1. Show your poster (on screen, hold up, or display)
2. Walk through your AI examples
3. Explain what you learned
4. Share your question about AI
5. Answer questions
6. Be proud of what you created!

**Remember:**
- Everyone gets a turn
- Be supportive of each other
- Ask questions
- Celebrate everyone's work!

### Part 2: Gallery Walk (15 minutes)

Look at each other's posters!

**What to do:**
- Walk around and see all the posters
- Discover new AI examples you didn't think of
- Give positive feedback
- Celebrate creativity!

**Try to:**
- Look at every poster
- Find at least 3 new AI examples
- Notice different creative approaches
- Appreciate everyone's work!

### Part 3: Badge Ceremony (10 minutes)

Earn your AI Curious badge! 🤖

**What you've earned:**
- You learned what AI is
- You found AI everywhere in your life
- You understood how AI learns
- You created an amazing poster
- You're AI Curious!

**Badge Requirements:**
- ✅ Completed Term 5
- ✅ Created AI in My Life poster
- ✅ Identified 5+ AI examples
- ✅ Explained what AI does
- ✅ Presented your work

**Congratulations!** 🎉
