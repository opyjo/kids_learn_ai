### Step 2: List Your AI Examples

Think about:
- What AI do you use every day?
- What AI did you discover this term?
- What AI surprised you?

**Examples to consider:**
- YouTube, Netflix, TikTok
- Siri, Alexa, Google Assistant
- Video games

- One question about AI

## 💻 Option 1: Python Program Version

If you choose to make a Python program:

```python
# ================================
# AI IN MY LIFE
# By: [Your Name]
# Term 5 Project
# ================================

print("=" * 50)
print("     🤖 AI IN MY LIFE 🤖")
print("=" * 50)
print("")

print("Here are 5+ examples of AI I use:")
print("")

print("1. YOUTUBE")
print("   What it does: Recommends videos I might like")
print("   Is it helpful? Yes! It helps me find cool videos")
print("")

print("2. SIRI")
print("   What it does: Answers my questions")
print("   Is it helpful? Yes! It helps me get information quickly")
print("")

print("3. VIDEO GAMES")
print("   What it does: Smart characters that adapt")
print("   Is it helpful? Yes! Makes games more fun")
print("")

print("4. AUTOCORRECT")
print("   What it does: Fixes my spelling mistakes")
print("   Is it helpful? Yes! Helps me type correctly")
print("")

print("5. NETFLIX")
print("   What it does: Suggests shows I might like")
print("   Is it helpful? Yes! Helps me find good shows")
print("")

print("-" * 50)
print("MY QUESTION ABOUT AI:")
print("How does AI learn so fast?")
print("=" * 50)
print("")
print("AI helps me every day!")
print("=" * 50)
```
