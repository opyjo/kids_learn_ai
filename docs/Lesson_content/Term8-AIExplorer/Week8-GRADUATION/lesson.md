## 🎤 How to Present Your Quiz Game

### What to Include (2-3 minutes)

1. **Introduce Your Game**
   - Your name
   - Your quiz theme
   - What makes it special

2. **Show Your Welcome Screen**
   - Run your game
   - Show the welcome message

3. **Demonstrate Gameplay**
   - Show a few questions
   - Show how answers are checked
   - Show score tracking
   - Show final results

4. **Share What You're Proud Of**
   - What was your favorite feature?
   - What was challenging?
   - What did you learn?

### Presentation Tips

- **Speak clearly** — Take your time
- **Be proud** — You created something amazing!
- **Have fun** — This is a celebration!
- **Answer questions** — Your classmates might ask about your game

---

## 🎤 How to Present Your AI Topic

### What to Include (2-3 minutes)

1. **Introduce Your Topic**
   - Your name
   - Your AI topic
   - Why you chose it

2. **Explain What the AI Does**
   - What does it do?
   - How does it work?
   - What problem does it solve?

3. **Explain the Data**
   - What data does it use?
   - How does it use lists?
   - How does it process information?

4. **Share Cool Facts**
   - What's interesting about it?
   - What surprised you?
   - What makes it special?

5. **Share Concerns or Questions**
   - What concerns do you have?
   - What questions do you have?
   - What should we think about?

### Presentation Tips

- **Speak clearly** — Take your time
- **Be confident** — You researched and learned!
- **Have fun** — Share your curiosity!
- **Answer questions** — Your classmates might ask about your topic
