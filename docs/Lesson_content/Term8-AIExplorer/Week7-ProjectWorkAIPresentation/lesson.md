## 📝 Presentation Requirements

### Must Include

1. **What the AI Does**
   - Explain what the AI system does
   - How does it work?
   - What problem does it solve?

2. **What Data It Might Use**
   - What information does the AI use?
   - Where does the data come from?
   - How does it use lists of data?

3. **Something Cool About It**
   - What's interesting or amazing?
   - What makes it special?
   - What surprised you?

4. **One Concern or Question**
   - What concerns do you have?
   - What questions do you have?
   - What should we think about?

### Presentation Format

You can create your presentation in any format:
- **Slides** (Google Slides, PowerPoint)
- **Poster** (drawing or digital)
- **Written document** (essay or report)
- **Video** (record yourself explaining)
- **Your choice!** (be creative!)

---

## 🎯 Step-by-Step Guide

### Step 1: Choose Your Topic

Think about:
- Which topic interests you most?
- Which one do you want to learn about?
- Which one can you explain to others?

**Choose one!**

### Step 2: Research Your Topic

Find out:
- What does this AI do?
- How does it work?
- What data does it use?
- What's cool about it?
- What concerns or questions are there?

**Use:**
- Ask your instructor
- Search online (with parent help)
- Think about what you know
- Use your imagination!

### Step 3: Plan Your Presentation

Think about:
- How will you explain what it does?
- How will you explain the data?
- What's the coolest thing to share?
- What concern or question will you include?

### Step 4: Create Your Presentation

Create it in your chosen format:
- Slides: Make 4-6 slides
- Poster: Draw or create digitally
- Document: Write 1-2 pages
- Video: Record 2-3 minutes
- Your way: Be creative!

### Step 5: Practice Presenting

Practice:
- Speaking clearly
- Explaining your topic
- Answering questions
- Being confident!

---

## 💡 Tips for Success

### Tip 1: Choose What Interests You

Pick a topic you're curious about!

### Tip 2: Keep It Simple

You don't need to know everything—just share what you learn!

### Tip 3: Be Creative

Make your presentation fun and engaging!

### Tip 4: Practice

Practice presenting so you're confident!

### Tip 5: Ask for Help

If you're stuck, ask your instructor or parent!
