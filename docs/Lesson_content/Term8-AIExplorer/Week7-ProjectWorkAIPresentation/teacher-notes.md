# Term 8, Lesson 7: Project Work - AI Presentation 🤖

## Teacher's Guide

**Course:** Term 8: AI Explorer  
**Age Group:** 9-10 years old  
**Duration:** 60 minutes  
**Term:** 8 of 8 | **Week:** 7 of 8

---

## 📋 Lesson Overview

### Purpose

This is Part B of the graduation project. Students create a presentation about an AI topic of their choice. This allows them to explore real AI systems, think critically about them, and practice presentation skills. This prepares them for graduation next week.

### Learning Objectives

By the end of this lesson, students will be able to:

1. Choose an AI topic that interests them
2. Research and understand an AI system
3. Create a presentation about their topic
4. Include required elements (what it does, data, cool facts, concerns)
5. Practice presenting for graduation

### Materials Needed

- Computer with internet access (for each student)
- Projector/screen share for demonstrations
- Optional: Presentation software access
- Optional: Art supplies for posters
- Whiteboard for topic discussion

### Pre-Lesson Preparation

1. **Review AI topics** — Understand each option
2. **Prepare research resources** — Age-appropriate resources
3. **Think about presentation formats** — What works best
4. **Prepare guidance** — How to help students choose
5. **Plan for different formats** — Slides, posters, videos, etc.

## 📚 Detailed Teaching Guide

### Part 1: Topic Introduction (10 minutes)

#### Goals

- Explain all AI topics
- Help students choose
- Build excitement

#### Script/Talking Points

> "Welcome to Part B of your graduation project! 🤖 Today, you're going to choose an AI topic, research it, and create a presentation. This is your chance to explore real AI systems!"

**Present Each Topic:**

Go through each option:
1. Netflix/YouTube recommendations
2. Voice assistants (Siri/Alexa)
3. AI in video games
4. Self-driving cars
5. AI art generators
6. AI and animals

For each, briefly explain:
- What it is
- Why it's interesting
- What they might learn

**Help Students Choose:**

> "Think about: Which one interests you most? Which one do you want to learn about? Which one can you explain to others?"

Give students time to think and choose. Walk around and help.

#### Teaching Tips

- Make each topic sound exciting
- Help students find their interest
- Encourage questions
- Be flexible if students have other ideas

---

### Part 2: Research & Planning (20 minutes)

#### Goals

- Students research their topic
- Plan their presentation
- Think about required elements

#### Activity: Research

> "Now let's research! Find out:"
- What does this AI do?
- How does it work?
- What data does it use?
- What's cool about it?
- What concerns or questions are there?

**Provide Resources:**
- Guide students to age-appropriate resources
- Help with research
- Answer questions
- Encourage note-taking

#### Activity: Planning

> "Now let's plan! Think about:"
- How will you explain what it does?
- How will you explain the data?
- What's the coolest thing to share?
- What concern or question will you include?

Help students plan their presentations.

#### Teaching Tips

- Guide research
- Help with note-taking
- Encourage planning
- Support different learning styles

---

### Part 3: Creating Presentation (25 minutes)

#### Goals

#### Support Different Formats

**For Posters:**

**For Documents:**

**For Videos:**

**For Other Formats:**
- Support creativity
- Ensure requirements met

#### Teacher Circulation

- Check progress
- Help with content

#### Teaching Tips

- Support different formats
- Ensure all requirements included
- Encourage creativity
- Help as needed

## 🎯 Assessment & Differentiation

### Formative Assessment

**During class, observe:**

- [ ] Did student choose a topic?
- [ ] Is student researching effectively?
- [ ] Is student planning presentation?
- [ ] Is student including required elements?
- [ ] Is student creating something engaging?

### Project Assessment: "AI Presentation"

| Criteria              | Exceeds (3)                    | Meets (2)                  | Developing (1)           |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------ | -------------------------- | ------------------------ |
| **What AI does**       | Clear, detailed explanation    | Basic explanation          | Unclear or missing       |
| **Data used**          | Thoughtful, detailed           | Basic understanding        | Unclear or missing       |
| **Cool fact**          | Engaging, interesting          | Basic fact                 | Missing or unclear       |
| **Concern/question**   | Thoughtful, critical thinking   | Basic concern/question     | Missing or unclear       |
| **Creativity**         | Highly creative, engaging     | Some creativity            | Little creativity        |
| **Presentation quality** | Well-organized, clear         | Organized                  | Disorganized            |

### Differentiation Strategies

#### For Students Who Need More Support

- Help choose simpler topic
- Provide research guidance
- Help with structure
- Pair with peer
- Reduce to essential elements

#### For Advanced Students

- Challenge with complex topics
- Encourage deeper research
- Ask critical questions
- Have them help peers
- Encourage advanced formats

## ⚠️ Common Challenges & Solutions

### Research Challenges

| Problem                  | Signs                          | Solution                                    |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| Overwhelmed by research  | Too much information           | Guide to key points, simplify               |
| Can't find information   | Struggling to research         | Provide resources, help search             |
| Too complex              | Information too advanced       | Simplify, focus on basics                  |

### Presentation Challenges

| Problem                  | Signs                          | Solution                                    |
| ------------------------ | ------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------- |
| Don't know format        | Confused about how to create   | Help choose format, provide examples        |
| Missing requirements      | Not including all elements     | Use checklist, guide through               |
| Not creative             | Basic, unengaging              | Encourage creativity, provide ideas        |
