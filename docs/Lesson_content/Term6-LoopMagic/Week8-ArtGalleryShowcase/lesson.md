## 🎤 How to Present Your Project

### What to Include (2-3 minutes)

1. **Introduce Yourself**
   - Your name
   - What you created

2. **Show Your Welcome Screen**
   - Run your program
   - Show the menu

3. **Demonstrate Your Patterns**
   - Choose at least 2 patterns
   - Show them running
   - Explain what makes them special

4. **Share What You're Proud Of**
   - What was your favorite part?
   - What was challenging?
   - What did you learn?

### Presentation Tips

- **Speak clearly** — Take your time
- **Be proud** — You created something amazing!
- **Have fun** — This is a celebration!
- **Answer questions** — Your classmates might ask about your project

### Example Script

> "Hi! I'm [Your Name], and I created an ASCII Art Generator! It has [number] patterns that users can choose from. Let me show you..."

[Run your program and demonstrate]

> "I'm really proud of [specific feature] because [reason]. The hardest part was [challenge], but I learned [what you learned]."

## 🎨 Gallery Walk

### What is a Gallery Walk?

After presentations, you'll walk around and:
- See everyone's projects
- Try out different ASCII Art Generators
- Give compliments
- Learn from each other

### How to Give Feedback

When you see a classmate's project:

- **Be positive** — "I love your patterns!"
- **Be specific** — "Your diamond pattern is really cool!"
- **Ask questions** — "How did you make that pattern?"
- **Be encouraging** — "Great job!"
