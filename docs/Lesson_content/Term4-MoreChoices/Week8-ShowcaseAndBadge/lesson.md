## 🎮 Showcase Activities

### Part 1: Presentations (30 minutes)

Each student will present their adventure game!

**What to share:**
- Your adventure's theme
- How it starts
- What choices players make
- How many endings it has
- Your favorite part
- What you're most proud of

**How to present:**
1. Run your adventure game
2. Show one path through the game
3. Explain your choices and endings
4. Answer questions
5. Be proud of what you built!

**Remember:**
- Everyone gets a turn
- Be supportive of each other
- Ask questions
- Celebrate everyone's work!

### Part 2: Playing Games (20 minutes)

Play each other's adventures!

**What to do:**
- Try different paths in each game
- See all the different endings
- Give positive feedback
- Celebrate creativity!

**Try to play:**
- At least 3 different adventures
- Different paths in each one
- See how creative everyone was!

### Part 3: Badge Ceremony (10 minutes)

Earn your Decision Master badge! 🏆

**What you've earned:**
- You learned if, else, elif
- You learned and, or
- You built menus and quizzes
- You created an amazing adventure game
- You're a Decision Master!

**Badge Requirements:**
- ✅ Completed Term 4
- ✅ Built expanded adventure game
- ✅ Used elif for multiple choices
- ✅ Created 4+ endings
- ✅ Added scoring/inventory
- ✅ Presented your work

**Congratulations!** 🎉
