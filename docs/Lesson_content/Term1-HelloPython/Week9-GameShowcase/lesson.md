## 🚀 Term 2 Preview: What's Coming Next!

Get excited — Term 2 is going to be AMAZING!

### New Superpower: if/else

Right now, your Mad Libs story is always the same — it just fills in different words.

What if the story could **CHANGE** based on what the player types?

```python
choice = input("Do you want a scary or funny story? ")
# What if the story CHANGED based on the answer?
# That's Term 2...
```

With `if/else`, your program can make **decisions**:

```python
# This is coming in Term 2!
mood = input("Are you feeling brave or silly today? ")
if mood == "brave":
    print("A knight rode into battle on a very serious horse...")
else:
    print("A penguin moonwalked into the supermarket...")
```

The same program — completely different stories based on the player's choice!

### New Superpower: Loops

```python
# Term 2 also brings... loops!
for i in range(3):
    print("Story round", i + 1)
    # ... collect words and reveal a story
    # ... then do it again, automatically!
```

Loops mean your game can run multiple stories without you writing the code twice!
